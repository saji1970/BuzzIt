Data Deletion Request

Request deletion of your BuzzIt account and data

What will be deleted?

When you submit this request, we will delete:

  • Your account information (username, email, phone number)
  • Your profile data (profile picture, bio, settings)
  • All your Buzz posts (text, images, videos)
  • Your comments and interactions
  • Connected social media accounts (Facebook, Instagram, etc.)
  • Your live stream history
  • All other personal data associated with your account

Warning: This action cannot be undone. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days.

Request Submitted Successfully

Your data deletion request has been received. We will process your request within 30 days. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is complete.

Request Failed

An error occurred while submitting your request. Please try again or contact support.
